About me

My research is concerned with applying new information technologies to solve construction management problems. I am particularly interested in construction simulation modelling, building information modelling (BIM), digital twins and gaming technologies. I completed my PhD in the Centre for Structural Engineering and Informatics at the University of Nottingham. I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Thomas Ashton Institute at the University of Manchester. Currently, I am an Assistant Professor in Construction Management at the University of Nottingham.

Research interests

  • Building Information Modelling
  • Construction simulation
  • Digital twins in the construction industry
  • Construction health and safety
  • Object-oriented modelling
  • Virtual reality
  • Serious games
